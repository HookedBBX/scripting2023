A Gist of Who I am


On August 14, 1996, a not so celebrated child was born, at a simple yet happy province of Guiguinto, Bulacan, Philippines. His name is Victor Avelino V. De Guzman. He lives with his father Avelino De Guzman, his mother Victoria De Guzman, his sister Anna Victoria De Guzman and his brother Ronald De Guzman. They have a happy family, living in a simple house yet filled with love and affection.

In his early years, he was exposed to music because of his father’s side job as a singer. Every day, he hears his father practice singing and playing the guitar and it even helps him sleep whenever he sings the “Desperado” by the well-known band Eagles. By the evening, when his brother Ronald comes home from college, he often hears him playing the piano. This explains how he developed learning piano at the age of 5 and played his debut performance in an established bank named Bank of the Philippine Islands on Christmas.

Music became the foundation of Victor’s life. He keeps on discovering his talents to the point that he finds out another talent of his. It is called Human Beatboxing. After learning the basics, he didn’t stop the momentum and reached his prime resulting to straight 5 wins on Human Beatbox Competitions in the Philippines. He even joined International Competitions Online and won some tournaments. He loved Beatboxing so much and the community in the Philippines.

On January 09, 2023, he got married to Angel Lee B. Aclan. From there, they started building their future from scratch, and finally decided to migrate to Lethbridge, Alberta, Canada. Leaving their families was hard but on August 11, 2023, they finally left their home country Philippines and started a new life in Canada.

They are both currently employed, and Victor is balancing both his job and his studies. Despite their hectic schedules, they continue to call and be available for their family in the Philippines to maintain contact. Both Victor and Angel’s vision and goals are to help their families back in the Philippines and build a brighter future for their future children.
